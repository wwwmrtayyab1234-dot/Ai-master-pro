Delete your AI Master Pro account

You can permanently delete your account and associated local app data inside AI Master Pro:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Select Delete Account & Local Data.
  3. Review the warning and confirm deletion.

This removes the Firebase account and local chats, memories, attachments, and usage data stored by the app on that device. Provider-side retention may follow each provider's own policy.
