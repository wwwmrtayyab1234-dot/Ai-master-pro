Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This policy explains how AI Master Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use its AI chat, image generation, voice generation, speech-to-text, and file-analysis features.

Release requirement: replace support@your-domain.example below with your real support address before publishing this page or submitting the app to Google Play.

Information we process

  • Account data: email address, Firebase user ID, verification status, and authentication tokens used to keep you signed in.
  • User content: prompts, chat messages, images, documents, audio, and video that you choose to submit for AI processing.
  • App data: chat history, saved preferences, credit balance, request counts, and feature usage stored for your account.
  • Microphone data: audio is captured only after you tap the microphone control and grant permission. It is used to create the requested speech-to-text transcript.
  • Diagnostics: limited technical error information may be processed to maintain reliability. Do not intentionally include confidential information in feedback.
  • Advertising data: Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, ad impressions, clicks, reward completion, and diagnostic information to deliver, measure, secure, and prevent fraud in ads. The app requests non-personalized ads by default.

How information is used

We process information to authenticate users, provide requested AI results, remember user-requested preferences, enforce per-account usage limits, restore chats, prevent abuse, provide support, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal information.

Service providers

Depending on the feature you choose, content may be sent to service providers needed to complete your request, including Google Firebase for authentication, Google Gemini for multimodal analysis and speech transcription, Groq for text generation, Pollinations for image generation, Microsoft Edge TTS for free voice generation, and ElevenLabs for premium voice generation. Google AdMob supplies rewarded and native advertisements and processes advertising and anti-fraud data. Their processing is also governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Advertising and rewards

The free version displays native and rewarded ads supplied by Google AdMob. A credit reward is granted only after all required rewarded ads are completed and the mobile advertising SDK reports the completion event. Closing an ad early does not grant progress. AI prompts, chats, generated images, documents, audio, and video are not intentionally sent to AdMob for ad targeting.

Storage and retention

Chat history, memory, attachments, and usage state are stored locally on the device in this MVP and separated by authenticated Firebase user ID. Authentication refresh credentials use secure device storage when available. Submitted content may also be retained temporarily by an AI provider under that provider's policies. We retain local data until you delete it, delete your account and local data in Settings, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

Account and data deletion

You can delete your Firebase account and local chats, memories, attachments, and usage data inside the app at Settings → Delete Account & Local Data. If you cannot access the app, request deletion at support@your-domain.example. Requests may require identity verification.

Permissions

The app uses internet access for authentication and AI services. It uses the operating system's file picker for files you explicitly select. Microphone permission is requested only when speech-to-text is used. The app does not request camera or location permission.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as verified Firebase authentication, secure token storage where supported, HTTPS provider connections, account-scoped local data, and restricted Android permissions. No electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If local law requires parental consent at a higher age, users must meet that requirement. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate authorization.

Your choices

You may avoid uploading files, deny microphone permission, export or import chats, delete individual chats, log out, or delete your account and local data. Contact us to ask questions about access, correction, or deletion rights available in your region.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features, providers, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top will be updated, and material changes may also be announced in the app.
